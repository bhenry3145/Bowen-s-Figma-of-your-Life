This was supposed to be a triathlon, but unfortunately the water in the lake had some kind of bacteria contamination so we were not allowed to swim. They replaced the 200m swim a mile run. The first section was a 1/2 mile sprint, then a 4 mile bike ride, and then finally with a mile run. I finished 10th out of 80 people, despite riding my dad's 50 lb mountain bike, while everyone else had their own super-light racing bikes. I trained for this for many months, so it was very satisfying to finish with good times.
